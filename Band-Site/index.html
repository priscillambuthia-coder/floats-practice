Who is KATSEYE?

KATSEYE (캣츠아이) is a multinational K-pop-style global girl group formed by HYBE and Geffen Records through the survival show The Debut: Dream Academy, debuting in June 2024 with members from diverse backgrounds (Philippines, South Korea, US, Switzerland, Ghana), known for their polished performances, hybrid pop sound blending K-pop precision with Western accessibility, and building a strong global fandom through their unique journey documented on Netflix. Formation & Concept Hybrid Model: KATSEYE embodies a new pop model, merging K-pop's rigorous training and performance standards with Western pop's global appeal, singing in English. Global Auditions: Formed from over 120,000 applicants, the six members were selected via The Debut: Dream Academy, an intense program showcased on YouTube and Netflix. Producers: A joint venture between HYBE (BTS's agency) and Geffen Records (Interscope), aiming to create a boundary-transcending group. Members Sophia Laforteza: Leader, Filipino-American (Philippines/USA), strong vocals, ukulele/piano. Daniela Avanzini: American with Venezuelan/Cuban roots, main dancer. Megan Skiendiel: Hawaiian (Singaporean Chinese mother), all-rounder. Manon Bannerman: Swiss-Ghanaian, multilingual, songwriter. Lara Raj: Indian-American, represents diverse cultures. Yoonchae Jeong: Youngest member, from South Korea.
